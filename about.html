Who We Are

Northville4Bees is a community-driven organization dedicated to supporting pollinators and building a healthier environment for future generations within our community. Founded by local highschool students passionate about conservation, our mission is simple: empower Northville residents to make a real difference — one seed, one garden, and one bee at a time.

Through educational outreach, eco-friendly products, and community events, we’re helping transform ordinary backyards, schools, and parks into thriving habitats where pollinators can flourish.

Our Mission

At Northville4Bees, we believe small actions create big change. Our mission is to:

  • Raise awareness about the critical role of bees and pollinators
  • Provide easy, affordable ways for people to create bee-friendly gardens
  • Strengthen the Northville community through shared environmental action

Our Story

Northville4Bees was founded in 2025 by three students — Moritz, Caleb, and Ayan — who saw a need for real, local action to protect pollinators. Starting as a small club project, our movement grew from a simple idea into a mission to make Northville a leading example of a pollinator-friendly community. Today, we're proud to work with local families, businesses, schools, and volunteers who share our vision for a greener, healthier world.
